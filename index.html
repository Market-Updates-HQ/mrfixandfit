How Can We Help You?

Whether it's a quick fix or a major project, we're here to make sure your home is in top shape.

Handyman

Our handyman services cover everything from fixing small problems like squeaky hinges and assembling flat-pack furniture to tackling bigger projects like kitchen upgrades. We can also help with painting, installing TV mounts, fixing broken fixtures and fittings, and even outdoor work like fixing and replacing guttering.

Plumbing

Our plumbing services cater to a wide range of needs, whether it's fixing small inconveniences like leaky taps and drain blockages, or addressing larger concerns such as replacing faulty radiators. We also take pride in taking on more substantial projects like bathroom upgrades and installations.

Fix & Fit

Whether it's a quick fix or a more major task, we're here to make sure your home is in top shape and our service suits your needs perfectly. Everything from hanging mirrors or picture frames to replacing doors, fitting locks or handles. If the job you need done is possible, then we will definitely consider it.

💡 Did You Know?

If you need an extra set of hands or a bit of extra muscle for a few hours or even for an entire day, we are able to assist — and we offer this as one of our many services.
